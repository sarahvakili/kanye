Yeezus Fun Facts

Welcome to the Yeezus Fun Facts page where you can learn 10 fun facts about the rapper everyone loves to hate, Kanye West!

Ready for those fun facts?

  1. The name Kanye means "the only one" in Swahilli.
  2. Kanye dropped out of college to pursue music which is why he named his 2004 debut album, The College Dropout.
  3. His single debut is "Through the Wire" because he recorded it while he was still wearing the metal brace in his mouth.
  4. He is a huge fan of Fiona Apple and her music.
  5. In December 2006, Robert "Evel" Knievel sued West for trademark infringement in West's video for "Touch the Sky."
  6. His song "Stronger" famously used a sample of Daft Punk's "Harder, Better, Faster, Stronger". But Kanye has also created some unreleased songs that contain samples from Broadway hit music, "Wicked".
  7. West is one of the best-selling artists of all time, having sold more than 21 million albums and 100 million digital downloads.
  8. In August 2015, during the MTV Video Music Awards ceremony, West announced his candidacy for President of the United States in the 2020 election.
  9. An episode of South Park depicting Kanye as an egomaniac is said to have "hurt his feelings".
  10. Kanye's net worth is estimated at around $120 million.
